Robotic Residency Curriculum

Staten Island University is committed to the future healthcare providers' training at our institution. As more surgeons and patients request procedures are performed robotically, it is important we refine our curriculum to best prepare our future surgeons. With 4 da Vinci robotic systems across our system, residents will have unfettered access to learn the fundamentals of robotic surgery and develop their technical skill set. In 2023, Northwell committed to offering innovative solutions to their training program and supplemented each da Vinci system with the Hub technology. Leveraging our talented program directors, world class robotic surgeons Northwell Health offers residents a state-of-the-art training program and curriculum.

  • PGY 1 - 5 Requirements (including observation of 15 cases, 5 cholecystectomies and 10 hernia repairs
  • PGY 2 - 4 Requirements (including bed side assisting in 10 cases and competency assessment
  • PGY 3 - 6 Requirements (including participation in 15 robotic cases at the discretion of surgical attending.
  • PGY 4 - 5 Requirements with increasing participation in complex cases and observation of complex cases
  • PGY 5 - 3 Requirements with completion of >50% of cases as primary surgeon on console
